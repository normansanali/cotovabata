Kurt Sutter Wiki Biography

Kurt Sutter he was born on the 5th May, 1960 in Rahway, New Jersey, USA, and is a well known director, screenwriter, producer as well as an actor. He is the creator of the series “Sons of Anarchy” (2008 – 2014), in which he also played an incarcerated member of the motorcycle club, as well as creator of other popular television series. Kurt Sutter has been accumulating his net worth being active in the entertainment industry since 2002.

How much is the net worth of Kurt Sutter? As of the data given in 2016, his wealth is reported to be equal to $10 million, accumulated from his career in the film industry.

Kurt Sutter Net Worth $10 Million

Sutter was raised in Clark, New Jersey in a family with two elder sisters. His mother worked as a secretary at the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Newark whereas his father worked for General Motors. In 1982, Kurt matriculated from Roselle Catholic High School and continued his studies at Livingston College of Rutgers University. In 1986, he graduated with a Bachelors degree in mass media. For some time he studied Meisner technique, worked in theatre as well as taught students at the Conservatory of Gately and Poole. In 1997, he graduated from Northern Illinois University having a Masters degree in performance and directing.

In 2002, Sutter began his career in television which later evolved into a very successful and profitable one. At first Kurt worked as a screenwriter for the television series “The Shield” and only tried other positions after some years, including as producer of the same series. In 2007, he also made his directorial debut staging of an episode of the previously mentioned series. In addition to this, he also landed a support role in the same television series.

Since 2008 he has devoted himself primarily to the crime drama television series “Sons of Anarchy” (2008 – 2014). He is the creator of the series, and directed six episodes. In the series, he played the imprisoned and nearly blind gang member Otto who was active in 13 episodes. In 2012, he worked in the documentary series “Outlaw Empires”, involved as executive producer, a series which focuses on the lives of American outlaws. In 2015, the historical fiction drama series “The Bastard Executioner” was started on FX; Kurt Sutter is the creator, executive producer as well as an actor playing in a supporting role. Overall, directing, creating and producing the series has increased the net worth of Kurt Sutter considerably.

What is more, Sutter is currently working on the comic book series “Lucas Stand”, which is going to be released by Boom! Studios. According to the media reports, it is going to be released on the 1st June 2016.

Finally, in the personal life of the director, producer, writer and actor, Sutter has been married to the actress Katey Sagal since 2004, and they have a daughter born in 2007, with the help of a surrogate mother. Kurt Sutter has two stepchildren from the previous marriage of his wife. Currently, the family resides in Los Feliz, California.
