One Punch Man Chapter 200 Release Date Revealed: Saitama and Garou's Epic Showdown Takes Center Stag

July 2024 · 4 minute read

Fans are eagerly anticipating the release of One Punch Man chapter 200 next week on Thursday, January 18, 2024. However, considering the past breaks in the manga and magazine schedule, it appears that the release will be delayed by two weeks, now expected on Thursday, January 25, 2024.

This means that manga enthusiasts will have to patiently wait for almost a month since the last chapter was published. It’s worth noting that Yusuke Murata, the creator of the manga, recently announced the establishment of his new animation studio. The break in the release schedule might be a valuable time for him to focus on this new project.

Adding to the reasons for the delay, Murata shared that he recently caught a cold. Therefore, taking a break will not only allow fans to wait for the next chapter but also give Murata the necessary time to rest and recover from his illness.

Saitama will unleash his mighty punch, but Garou will skillfully dodge it and counter with his own powerful techniques like the Cross Fang Dragon Slayer Fist and the God Slayer Instant Attack.

But the action doesn’t stop there. The chapter will also dive into the reactions and fates of other important characters witnessing the battle, including Bang, Bomb, Genos, Fubuki, King, Tatsumaki, Gyoro Gyoro, Orochi, and Black Sperm. Some may jump into the fray to help, while others might suffer injuries or even lose their lives due to the chaos.

Furthermore, get ready for some deep insights into Garou’s past, motivations, and beliefs. The chapter will explore his connection with Bang, who used to be his teacher. Garou will open up about his intense hatred for heroes and his burning desire to become the most powerful monster, viewing Saitama as his ultimate nemesis.

As Saitama tries to understand Garou’s perspective, he’ll question his motives and choices. And hold on tight, because the upcoming chapter will throw unexpected twists your way. This might include new characters entering the scene, the Hero Association or Monster Association getting involved, unveiling hidden abilities or secrets, and the sudden appearance of an unforeseen danger or ally.

Recap One Punch Man Chapter 199

In Chapter 199 of One Punch Man, titled That’s Right, we get a brief but satisfying story. The focus is on Flashy Flash and Sonic as they take on the elite ninjas from the village, the henchmen of the mysterious “That Man.”

These arrogant ninjas refuse to hear any criticism about their boss when Flash presents some hard truths. Meanwhile, Brawny Muscle launches an attack called “Stone Debris Release,” a complex three-dimensional technique mastered only by the village’s top-tier ninjas.

As the relentless attacks keep coming, Flash devises a plan to defend himself. He thwarts the assault by destroying the entire foothold, surprising everyone and swiftly defeating the entire gang.

Flash emphasizes that strength doesn’t necessarily come from village techniques; it can manifest naturally if one is powerful enough. Despite being astonished by Flash’s incredible speed, Sonic hesitates to acknowledge his rival.

Flash makes it clear that victory comes not just from training but from combat experience. He adds that one cannot become stronger if they clings to their worthless pride. Sonic, while bleeding from both nostrils, reluctantly agrees. Despite his wounded pride, Flash acknowledges Sonic’s growth, hinting at possibly mending their rivalry.

The lingering question for fans is whether they will encounter “That Man” in the next chapter.
