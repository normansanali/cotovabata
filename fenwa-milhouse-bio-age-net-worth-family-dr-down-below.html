Meet Dr Down Below Fenwa Milhouse! His Age, Net Worth, Family

July 2024 · 5 minute read

Dr. Down Below is the new TLC special which follows a board-certified, fellowship-trained urologist Dr. Fenwa Milhouse as she investigates her patients who have issues “down below” in an hour-long episode. Learn about Dr. Fenwa in the wiki article below.

Fenwa Milhouse On TLC’s Dr Down Below

Dr. Down Below, which premiered on April 5th, 2023, is a one-episode special titled From the Waist Down but it would be no surprise if TLC orders more episodes. Star of the show, Dr. Fenwa Milhouse is a urologist and specialist in Female Pelvic Medicine and Reconstructive Surgery (also known as Urogynecology).

Episode 1’s synopsis read, “Urologist Dr. Fenwa Milhouse treats a woman suffering from a painful labial cyst and helps a man who struggles with an extra pee hole left by a Prince Albert piercing. Later, Dr. Milhouse sees a woman with an advanced stage vaginal prolapse.”

In the 1st episode, Fenwa at one point said that she chose to venture into the urology field because it’s not about penises and vaginas. Rather, it’s about quality of life as Fenwa tells. “No one knows what urology is. People think it’s for men, by men… That’s not true at all. Urology is a surgical specialty that deals with the urinary tract… But because everyone has a urinary tract, we deal with men and women. I deal with everything down below,” Fenwa said.

How Much Is Fenwa Milhouse Net Worth?

As of April 2023, Fenwa Milhouse’s net worth was estimated to be under $3 million.

Dr. Fenwa studied at the University of Texas at Austin, from where she graduated with a BS in Biology. Later, she got her doctorate degree from the University of Texas Medical School.

After getting her MD, Fenwa completed Urology Residency Program at the University of Chicago only after which she became a licensed physician.

Now, talking about her job details, Fenwa’s first job was as a physician at the University of Chicago Medical Center, where she joined in June 2008. Later, she began her work at Metro Urology as a Clinical Fellow. In August 2015, Fenwa also began working at DuPage Medical Group as a Urologist.

Currently, Fenwa practices at Down There Urology, which is a physician-owned and patient-centered Chicago Urology clinic with clinical expertise and a focus on treating men’s and women’s sexual health & pelvic floor conditions.

As per downthereurology.com, Dr. Milhouse specializes in the treatment of “sexual dysfunction, urinary incontinence, pelvic organ prolapse, and cosmetic genital enhancement, including PhalloFILL™ girth enhancement, vaginal perineoplasty, labia majora surgery, G-spot amplification, and labiaplasty.”

Talking about Fenwa’s achievements, the reality TV star was named Top Urologist in Chicago in 2023 by Women in Medicine and has been featured in different media outlets such as Yahoo Style, HuffPost, Essence Magazine, and CNN, to name a few.

Fenwa has her own website–Your Favorite Urologist, where she has revealed her story of how a Black woman urologist inspired her to pursue the same field during medical school.

Fenwa Milhouse Age

As per radaris.com, Fenwa was born in October 1982. That means her age was 40 as of 2023.

Fenwa Milhouse Family

Fenwa’s family consists of her parents and a sibling. Her parents are Simeon and Lara Famakinwa. Lara, 67, is a former Registered Nurse Supervisor at Intracare Hospital North. She studied at Lagos University Teaching Hospital. Fenwa’s father, Simeon, turned 69 years old in 2022.

On May 17, 2021, Fenwa’s parents celebrated their 40th wedding anniversary. Congratulating them, the urologist wrote, “Happy 40th Anniversary to two humans who made me… No marriage is perfect but they have been the epitome, the embodiment, the standard for which I look up to and strive towards,” on Instagram.

Furthermore, Fenwa has a brother named Olumayowa A Famakinwa, who turned 38 years old in 2022.

Is Fenwa Milhouse Married?

Fenwa Milhouse is married to her husband, Wilber Milhouse. The two married in 2012 while Fenwa is in her fifth year of practicing medicine.

As Fenwa revealed in loveandthesky.com, Wilber is a very supportive husband and he took care of most household stuff while Fenwa was away due to her job.

Wilber celebrates his birthday on the 23rd of July every year. The adorable couple celebrated Wilber’s birthday on July 23, 2021, together.

Fenwa and Wilber have one daughter together. Wilber has two separate kids of his own from his previous relationship. Talking about her daughter, Fenwa wrote, “Y’all, let me brag on my 7-year-old daughter for a moment. She finished her first CHAPTER book today all by herself… Homeschool parenting moment for the win!!!”

Fenwa Milhouse Height

Dr. Fenwa Milhouse stands at an average height of 5 feet 6 inches.
