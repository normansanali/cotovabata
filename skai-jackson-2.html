Career

Skai Jackson started her career as a child actress with a popular role in the movie titled Liberty Kid in the year 2007 when she was just 5 years old.

After that, she played a short recurring role in the popular television series titled Rescue Me in the year 2008.

In the year 2009, she made her appearance in a romantic-comedy film titled The Rebound.

In the same year 2009, she made her appearance in Nickelodeon’s animated series titled Bubble Guppies.

After that, she has played several cameo roles in various movies and television series like Team Umizoomi, Royal Pains, Boardwalk Empire, Arthur, and The Smurfs.

In the year 2011, she made her appearance in the popular sitcom series titled Jessie on Disney Channel. In the year 2013, she portrayed the role of Joetta Watson in the popular television film titled The Watsons Go To Birmingham and in the following year made her appearance in the animated series titled Ultimate Spider-Man.

In the year 2015, she made her appearance in the popular Disney Channel series titled Bunk’d, and after that, she rose to huge popularity. In the very next year, she made a guest appearance in the series titled K.C. Undercover. She has also appeared in a small recurring role in series like Marvel Rising Initiation, Marvel Rising Chasing Ghost, and Marvel Rising Battle of the Bands.

In the year 2019, she has been featured in the music video titled Panini by Lil Nas Xs. Later in the year, she made her debut as an author and released her first novel titled Reach for the Skai How To Inspire, Empower, and Clapback, and also worked as a voice-over artist in the animated series titled DreamWorks Dragons Rescue Riders.

In the year 2020, she made her appearance in the popular TV reality show titled Dancing With the Stars as a celebrity contestant and reached up to the semi-finals with her partner Alan Bersten.
