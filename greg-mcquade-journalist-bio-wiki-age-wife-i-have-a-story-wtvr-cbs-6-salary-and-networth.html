Greg McQuade Biography and Wiki

Greg McQuade is an American Emmy award-winning television journalist who currently anchors the CBS6 Weekend Morning show alongside meteorologist Mike Stone. He is also the producer of two weekly franchises “Heroes Among Us” on Thursdays and “I Have a Story” on Fridays.

Previously before he joined CBS 6 Greg was in Boston working at NECN as a weekend reporter in 1998. He covered a wide range of New England and national stories including the 2000 Democratic National Convention in Los Angeles. In 2006, Greg produced an award-winning two-part series on his search called, “Soldier In the Photograph”.

Greg McQuade WTVR

Previously before Greg began working at WTVR he was working at NECN as a weekend reporter. He covered a wide range of New England and national stories including the 2000 Democratic National Convention in Los Angeles. One of the most memorable moments during his tenure at NECN was interviewing former Russian leader Mikail Gorbachev and Buzz Aldrin, the second human to walk on the moon. One of the most heart-wrenching stories he ever had to cover was the deaths of six Worcester firefighters who lost their lives in a cold storage facility during a fire in December of 1999.

He moved to Bangor, Maine in 1997 for his first full-time on-air reporting job at WVII-TV. The job prepared him well for a career in broadcasting. In a small market like Bangor you work long hours, shoot the pictures, write the story, edit the video, and go live all while getting paid very little. However, he loved it! It was an experience filled with riches he cannot count. He will always cherish the memories and the life-long friends he made.
