Olivia Wilde Flustered By Jason Sudeikis' Lesbian Relations Question

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Jason’s in trouble! Olivia Wilde faced an extremely awkward line of questioning during her Watch What Happens Live appearance on Thursday, Oct. 15. During the Bravo show’s “Plead the Fifth” segment, host Andy Cohen aired previously taped questions from Wilde’s fiancé, Jason Sudeikis. 

The Horrible Bosses 2 actor, 40, got sexual, natch, asking her to name the “wildest” place she’s ever had sex, to which Wilde immediately pleaded the Fifth. He then asked, “Have you ever dipped into the lady pond in real life?”

“Oh my God!” Wilde, 31, squealed. “I mean, what’s going on?” 

Taking a sip of her drink, the mother of 17-month-old Otis tried to avoid the question, saying, “I appreciate all people.”
