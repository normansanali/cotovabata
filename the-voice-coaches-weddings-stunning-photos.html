'The Voice' coaches past and present enjoyed some unforgettable weddings

    Unlike the ever-changing line-up on 'The Voice', many of the coaches have been happily married to their respective spouses for years. In fact, current judge Gwen Stefani, 54, will forever be grateful to the NBC show because it is where she met her now husband, former coach Blake Shelton, 47. 

    But they are not the only ones who are enjoying wedded bliss away from the show. John Legend, 44, has been married to Chrissy Teigen, 37, for a decade, and former coaches like Adam Levine, 44, and Alicia Keys, 42, are also off the market.  

    Take a look back at their spectacular wedding days below...

    'The Voice' coaches mind-blowing weddings

    Gwen Stefani and Blake Shelton 

    Gwen Stefani and Blake Shelton met on '' in 2014 and began a relationship the following year. In 2020, Blake proposed with a large diamond ring inside a beautiful chapel – which he is said to have built as a declaration of his love for Gwen – on his Oklahoma ranch. 

    One year later, the couple returned to the scene of their engagement and exchanged vows in an intimate ceremony on the 1,300-acre ranch, which was officiated by their friend, and host, Carson Daly. 

    View post on Instagram 

    Gwen looked sensational in two bridal gowns designed by Vera Wang. The first was a white strapless multi-layered tulle dress that featured a veil embroidered with the names of her children Kingston, Zuma, and Apollo, as well as her new husband. 

    For the evening celebrations, the singer made a quick change into a "party dress" which was a strapless mini dress with fishnet tights and white boots. 

    View post on Instagram 

    Gwen described their wedding as "one of the greatest moments of my life" on the Tell Me About It podcast, adding: "It was beyond what I thought it was gonna be." 

    John Legend 

    John Legend proposed to his wife, Chrissy Teigen, in 2011 during a Christmas vacation in the Maldives, presenting her with a stunning cushion-cut diamond engagement ring set between two eternity bands. 

    They married on September 14, 2013, at the Villa Pizzo in Lake Como, Italy. However, it was a rocky start to their big day as they were reportedly forced to delay their nuptials after a storm held up John's family. Chrissy chose three Vera Wang dresses for her special day, and she looked phenomenal in each of them. 

    View post on Instagram 

    Her first dress was an ivory strapless open-back tulle skirted ball gown, which she wore for the ceremony. She then changed into an ivory mermaid gown with a micro-pleated bodice and sheared organza full skirt. Her final look of the evening was another strapless mermaid gown but this time in a crimson red hue. 

    View post on Instagram 

    The reception featured John serenading his new wife with his hit song, '' – which he penned for Chrissy – and a performance by Stevie Wonder. Guests also dined on a four-tiered carrot cake layered with cream cheese frosting. 

    Alicia Keys 

    Former ' coach Alicia Keys married her husband, Swizz Beatz, 45, at a private home on the beautiful French island of Corsica on 31 July 2010, two months after announcing their engagement. 

    The '' singer was seven months pregnant with her first child, Egypt, at the time and wore an elegant Vera Wang gown to accommodate her growing baby bump. 

    View post on Instagram 

    Alicia looked radiant in an ivory silk, georgette-draped dress that boasted a fitted waist and a loose skirt. She carried a bouquet of purple calla lilies and wore her hair in a chic bun with a bridal headpiece. 

    View post on Instagram 

    On their 13th wedding anniversary, Alicia penned a sweet tribute to her husband on Instagram, writing: "There are no amount of words that could describe how grateful we are!!! Our life together is crazy/ fun/ real/ beautiful/ Deep/ unique/ special!" Swizz meanwhile, wrote: "13 years with love I hope we find each other every lifetime!" 

    Nick Jonas 

    Nick Jonas, 31, and his wife Priyanka Chopra, 41, had a whirlwind relationship before they tied the knot with two ceremonies in India. The couple were dating for two months before the former '' coach popped the question in July 2018. 

    That December, they enjoyed a weekend of lavish festivities at the five-star Taj Umaid Bhawan Palace in Jodhpur. The wedding was split into two – a Christian ceremony to honor Nick's upbringing, which was held on Saturday – and a Hindu ceremony to honor Priyanka's Indian culture the following day. 

    View post on Instagram 

    "I love that our wedding is a religious mash-up," the bride exclusively told HELLO!. "We are taking beautiful traditions that we both grew up with and we are personalizing them in a way that makes sense for us." 

    For the first nuptials, Priyanka looked exquisite in a custom Ralph Lauren gown, which featured floral and scroll motifs and lace-effect tulle appliqués with scalloped sleeves and a high-neck collar. The hand-beaded and hand-embroidered gown also included mother-of-pearl paillettes, crystal seed beads, and Swarovski crystals. The look was topped off by a 75-foot-long tulle veil. 

    View post on Instagram 

    For the Hindu ceremony, Priyanka looked every inch the beautiful bride in a traditional Indian outfit by noted designer Sabyasachi Mukherji. She stunned in a deep red lehenga (a two-piece outfit consisting of a skirt and a coordinating top). 

    View post on Instagram 

    The striking garment featured heavy detailing of intricate embroidery and fine jewels as well as sequin work and was teamed with traditional gold jewelry and a flower garland. 

    Adam Levine 

    Adam Levine started dating Victoria's Secret model Behati Prinsloo, 35, in 2012. Their relationship appeared to be going strong but in February 2013, the couple split. However, it was short-lived as that July, the formercoach popped the question with a vintage ring from the 1930s. 

    They wed at Flora Farms in San Jose del Cabo, Mexico, in a ceremony officiated by '' actor Jonah Hill on July 19, 2014. Guests dined on organic pork chops, fresh fish, and beets, while Adam's band, Maroon 5, provided the entertainment, alongside Stevie Nicks and Sublime. 

    It took six years for Behati to share a photo of her wedding dress, which was designed by Alexander Wang. In honor of the designer's birthday in 2020, the model shared a photo posing in her dress for a last-minute fitting before the wedding.

    Even without her bridal hair and makeup, Behati looked incredible in her white floor-length frock which featured a plunging neckline and revealing side cutouts.
