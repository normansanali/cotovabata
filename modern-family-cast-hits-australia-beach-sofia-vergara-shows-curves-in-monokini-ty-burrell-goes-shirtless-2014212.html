Modern Family Cast Hits Australia Beach, Sofia Vergara Shows Curves

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Modern Family beach day! The cast of the hit ABC sitcom filmed scenes together at Bondi Beach in Sydney, Australia on Friday, Feb. 21. As expected, to play her sexy Colombian character Gloria Delgado-Pritchett, Sofia Vergara flaunted her famous curves in a skimpy monokini.

The 41-year-old actress showed some skin — and major cleavage — in the neon pink, orange, and green Agent Provacateur swimsuit. For a little modesty, Vergara also wore a long purple skirt worn low around her waist.

Vergara wasn’t the only Modern Family cast member, however, to show off their beach body. The actress was spotted splashing in the water with a shirtless Ty Burrell. The 46-year-old actor plays Phil Dunphy, who is married to Julie Bowen‘s character Claire Dunphy. He revealed his slim physique while only wearing swim trunks with red writing on it.

Vergara’s onscreen husband Ed O’Neill (Jay Pritchett), Sarah Hyland (Haley Dunphy), Ariel Winter (Alex Dunphy), Nolan Gould (Luke Dunphy) and Rico Rodriguez (Manny Delgado) were also seen filming on the beach. O’Neill, 67, left his shirt on for the scene, and Bowen, 43, wore a blue cover-up over her swimsuit.
