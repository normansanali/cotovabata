Giovanni Reyna- Professional Career

Reyna, a sports personality, grew up playing soccer and began his professional career in 2019 by playing with US youth teams, where he gained experience and improved his game. Later in 2019, he relocated to Germany and joined the academy of Borussia Dortmund. Through his play, he astounded both the coach and his teammates. He was promoted to the club’s main team a year later. In January 2020, he made his professional and Bundesliga debuts. He made his Bundesliga debut in the 72nd minute as a substitute against FC Augsburg, setting the record for the youngest American to play in the league.

On the 4th of February 2020, he scored the first goal of his entire career against Werder Bremen in the DFB-Pokal Round of 16 fixtures. He made it to the UEFA Champions League match against PSG on February 18th, where he put up Erling Haaland’s game-winning goal for Borussia Dortmund. Giovanni Reyna assisted on a goal against Borussia Monchengladbach in his Bundesliga debut. He later signed a long-term contract with Dortmund until 2025 in November 2020. In a triumph over Panama, Reyna scored his first international goal.
