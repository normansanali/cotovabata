Lily Allen (born May 2, 1985) is an English Musical Artist, Singer, Songwriter, and Social Media Personality from Hammersmith, London. She first gained immense popularity for her debut single “Smile”.

The song released on the 3rd of July 2006 which earned the number one position on the UK Singles Chart in July 2006. Furthermore, the track was part of her first studio album “Alright, Still”.

It came out on July 13, 2006, by Regal Recordings. The album even received a nomination for Best Alternative Music Album at the 50th Grammy Awards. Some of the other tracks of the Alright, Still are “Shame for You”, “Littlest Things”, “Take What You Take”, “Friend of Mine” etc.
