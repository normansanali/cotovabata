Sheriff Stephen Gibson Obituary, Glen Rose TX, Somervell Investigator Killed In Crash

July 2024 · 2 minute read
0SHARESShareTweet

Sheriff Stephen Gibson Obituary, Death – On Wednesday morning, the Somervell County Sheriff’s Department has confirmed that Sergeant Stephen Gibson passed away as a result of injuries sustained in a traffic accident. According to the Texas Department of Public Safety, Troopers from the Texas State Highway Patrol responded to a call about the collision at approximately 6:45 a.m. on State Highway 144, which is located approximately four miles south of Glen Rose.

According to the authorities, Sgt. Gibson was driving his investigator truck northbound on State Highway 144 when all of a sudden, a white Ford F-150 pickup truck that was travelling southbound lost control and collided with his vehicle. Texas Department of Public Safety reports that Sgt. Gibson was killed in the collision. Over the course of more than fifteen years, he worked as a peace officer. “The unflinching dedication of Sergeant Gibson to serving and safeguarding the community, as well as his remarkable career, will be remembered for all time without end. In a statement, the department added, “During this difficult time, the family and this office request privacy as we grieve the loss of this dedicated public servant.” This statement was released in response to the tragic news.

An official statement from the Somervell County Sheriff’s Office was published on Facebook on Wednesday afternoon. The statement read, “Today is a day that is very difficult to put into words.” It is quite difficult to find the appropriate words to describe today. A wonderful man has passed away today in this neighbourhood! Officer Stephen Gibson, Sergeant…! On Wednesday, December 13, 2023, the Somervell County Sheriff’s Office published the following message: The Texas Department of Public Safety is continuing their investigation into the accident. It was not possible to obtain any additional information at that time.
