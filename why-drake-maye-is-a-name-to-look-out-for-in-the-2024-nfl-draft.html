Why Drake Maye is a Name to Look Out for in the 2024 NFL Draft

July 2024 Â· 3 minute read

Drake Maye, the 21-year-old quarterback from the University of North Carolina, has garnered significant attention as a potential top prospect in the 2024 NFL Draft. Here's a comprehensive analysis of Maye's potential as a top prospect in the upcoming NFL Draft.

Background and Accomplishments

  • Size: Drake Maye stands at 6'4" and weighs 223 pounds, with an arm length of 32" and a hand size of 9".
  • Accomplishments: He has an impressive list of accolades, including being named Second-Team All-ACC (2023), First-Team All-ACC (2022), ACC Player of the Year (2022), and more.

Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths

  • Elite Arm Talent: Maye possesses the kind of arm strength necessary for making any throws required in an NFL offense.
  • Pocket Maneuverability: He demonstrates the ability to operate in the middle of the field with a PFF passing grade of 94.8 on throws between the college hashes, leading all FBS quarterbacks with at least 50 attempts.
  • Multi-Speed Pitcher: Maye can alter the pace and tempo of his throws based on the target window, making routine NFL throws at all three levels of the field.

Weaknesses

  • Inconsistent Lower-Body Mechanics: Maye's footwork at times causes his accuracy to suffer, resulting in throws sailing due to poor footwork.
  • Pressure Handling: He had a 19.6% pressure-to-sack rate last season, which is on the higher side of where a quarterback should ideally be.

PFF Grades and Stats

  • Overall PFF Grade: Maye received an impressive 90.6 overall PFF grade, showcasing his potential as a high-ceiling NFL starting quarterback.
  • Adjusted Completion Percentage: His 75.1% adjusted completion percentage ranked only 25th best in the FBS, indicating room for improvement in consistent accuracy.

Film Analysis

  • Arm Talent: Maye's arm talent allows his offense access to the entire field, showcasing the ability to drive passes into tight windows.
  • Mobility: He is comfortable throwing from the pocket and on the move, with the ability to create second-reaction throws out of structure and outside the pocket.

NFL Projection and Prospect Projection

  • NFL Projection: Maye is projected as a high-ceiling NFL starting quarterback who thrives as a vertical/deep passer, with opportunities to extend plays due to his mobility.
  • Prospect Projection: He is projected to be a Day 1 pro bowl caliber player, indicating his potential to make an immediate impact in the NFL.

Expert Analysis

  • Some analysts believe that Maye could potentially fall in the draft due to concerns about his inconsistent 2023 season. However, his average draft position in the PFF Mock Draft Simulator is 2.6, signifying his steady position as a top-five selection throughout the draft cycle.

Drake Maye's combination of elite arm talent, pocket maneuverability, and multi-speed pitching make him a standout prospect in the 2024 NFL Draft. While he has areas to improve, such as his footwork and handling of pressure, his potential as a high-ceiling NFL starting quarterback is evident. With the right coaching and development, Maye has the opportunity to make a significant impact in the NFL and establish himself as a top quarterback in the league. Therefore, it's clear that Drake Maye is a name to look out for in the 2024 NFL Draft.
