Keanu Reeves Anorexia Update: Does He Have Eating Disorder? Health Update

No, Keanu Reeves does not have Anorexia, a severe eating disorder characterized by restriction of food intake and sometimes even leads to death because of malnutrition (inadequate minerals in the body) if not adequately cared for.

However, all the news circulating on the Internet regarding the rumored Keanu Reeves Anorexia case is entirely baseless as he is completely healthy.

Keanu actively appears on interviews and TV Shows to promote his recent blockbuster franchise’s new movie “John Wick Chapter: 4,” He looks perfect in shape. 

He stands at 6 ft 1 in tall (1.86 m) and weighs approximately 79 kg (175 lbs), which is an ideal weight for a tall stature person.

Keanu Reeves’ anorexia rumors roots down to one of his movies, ‘To the Bone,’ based on a young 20-year-old Anorexic Girl, Allen, and her struggles with the disorder.

Many fans believed that Keanu played a part in the movie because the story was relatable to his own life; however, it’s not true, and he has not been diagnosed with Anorexia.

Keanu Reeves Family & Personal Life

Reeves started dating Jennifer Syme, David Lynch’s assistant whom he met at a Party for his band Dogstar in 1998, and in 1999, Syme gave birth to their stillborn child eight months into her pregnancy.

John Wick Star and Syme broke up and reconciled before Syme’s death in a car accident on April 2, 2001, caused by impaired driving and not wearing a seatbelt.

The couple had brunch together in San Francisco before her fatal car accident. Reeves later acted as a pallbearer during Syme’s funeral, where she was buried beside their deceased daughter.

He has been romantically associated with Brenda Davis, a filmmaker and a close friend of his, who is also the godfather to her child, and with China Chow, a model Actress.

Furthermore, Reeves and Alexandra Grant met at a dinner Party in 2009 and collaborated on two books before making their romantic relationship public in November 2019.
