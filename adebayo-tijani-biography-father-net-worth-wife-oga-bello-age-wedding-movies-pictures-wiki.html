Biography

Adebayo Tijani is a well-known Yoruba film actor, producer, and director, who has worked on films such as The New Patriots (2021), Iwa Pele (2006), and Igbeyin Ewuro (2009).

He was born in Agbole Alubata, Oyo State, on March 24, 1975. He is a well-respected veteran in the Nigerian movie industry, especially in the Yoruba genre.

Early life

Adebayo Tijani was born on the 24th of March in 1975. He hails from the village of Agbole Alubata, Oyo State, in Nigeria.

He is supposed to be from the Bata Drummers family, however, he has said that he does not know how to play the drums.

Education

Adebayo Tijani received his primary and secondary education in Oyo State, as well as his National Diploma in Survey and Geo-Formatics Engineering from the Federal School of Surveying in Oyo Town.

Career

When Adebayo Tijani was President of a Theatre Group in the early 2000s, he began acting and producing films.

He has remained active and relevant in the Yoruba film business since then, having produced a number of films.

In 2016, he co-starred in the romantic thriller, Oku Aye with Femi Phillips. Femi Adebayo, Toyin Afolayan, Idowu Philips, Wale Akerede, Bayo Adedeji, and others starring in the film, which was a huge success.

The film was shot in Nigeria and the United Kingdom and is about very important themes in today’s society. It was intensely emotional, with a lot of suspense and action.

Awards

Adebayo Tijani was a Yoruba award winner at the 2006 African Movie Academy Awards.

Muyiwa Ademola, the Nollywood superstar Adebayo Tijani, considers Muyiwa Ademola to be his mentor.

Social Media

Adebayo Tijani is a producer and writer with over 282k followers on Instagram.

Personal Life

After dating for almost three years, Adebayo Tijani married Folashade Adebayo, a lawyer, in 2012. In 2012, they welcomed their first child.

Net Worth

Adebayo Tijani is a well-known Yoruba personality who is currently one of the highest-paid directors, with a net worth of almost $700,000.
