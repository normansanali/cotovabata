When and where is the Champions League final 2024?

September 2024 · 4 minute read

The 2024 UEFA Champions League Final is scheduled to take place at Wembley Stadium in London, United Kingdom. This prestigious event will be the 69th season of Europe's elite club competition and the 32nd since it was renamed the UEFA Champions League. Here's a detailed overview of this highly anticipated football event.

Date and Kickoff Time

The final is set to be held on Saturday, June 1, 2024. The match is confirmed to kick off at 8 p.m. BST in the UK, 3 p.m. EDT in the USA and Canada, and 5 a.m. AEST in Australia. The kickoff times in various countries are as follows:
  • Australia: Sun, June 2, 5 a.m. AEST
  • Canada: Sat, June 1, 3 p.m. EDT
  • Hong Kong: Sun, June 2, 3 a.m. HKT
  • India: Sun, June 2, 12:30 a.m. IST
  • Malaysia: Sun, June 2, 3 a.m. MYT
  • New Zealand: Sun, June 2, 7 a.m. NZST
  • Singapore: Sat, June 1, 3 a.m. SST
  • UK: Sat, June 1, 8 p.m. BST
  • USA: Sat, June 1, 3 p.m. EDT

Venue: Wembley Stadium

Wembley Stadium, located in London, UK, will host the 2024 Champions League final. With a seating capacity of 90,000, Wembley Stadium is renowned for its historical significance and has previously hosted several major football events, including the UEFA Champions League finals of 2011 and 2013. This will mark the eighth time London has hosted a Champions League Final, with the last final in the city being held in 2013. In addition, Wembley Stadium is a frequent host of other significant football matches, such as the FA Cup, Carabao Cup, and the Community Shield.

Tournament Overview

The 2023/24 UEFA Champions League season has been an exciting journey for football enthusiasts, with teams from across Europe vying for the prestigious title. The group phase saw prominent clubs like Manchester City and Real Madrid advancing comfortably, while Paris Saint-Germain faced a tough battle to progress to the Round of 16. Notably, the knockout stages have been drawn, with Manchester City and Arsenal set to face Copenhagen and Porto, respectively. The draw for the quarterfinals is scheduled to take place on Friday, March 15, 2024.

Ticket Information

Tickets for the 2024 Champions League final will be available through the official ticketing portals of the two clubs involved in the game, as well as through UEFA. A ballot to purchase tickets through UEFA is set to open closer to the date of the final. Furthermore, applications for hospitality packages can be submitted through UEFA's official website. For the previous final in 2023, there were four categories of tickets available to supporters, with prices ranging from €70 to €690. The attendance for the 2023 final exceeded 95% of the Ataturk Stadium's capacity of 75,145, showcasing the immense popularity of the event.

Historical Significance of Wembley Stadium

Wembley Stadium holds a significant place in football history, having been the venue for numerous iconic matches and finals. It previously hosted the Euro 2020 final and was one of the venues for the tournament, featuring eight matches, including one of the semi-finals. The Football Association successfully bid for Wembley Stadium to host the 2023 final in celebration of the centenary of the opening of the original stadium in 1923. However, due to the postponement and relocation of the Euro 2020 final, subsequent Champions League finals were rescheduled, leading to the 2024 final being held at Wembley Stadium. This will be the third final to take place at the reconstructed stadium, further solidifying its status as a premier football venue.

The 2024 UEFA Champions League Final at Wembley Stadium promises to be a thrilling and unforgettable event for football fans worldwide. With a rich history and a capacity of 90,000, Wembley Stadium is set to host this prestigious final, which will see the culmination of the 69th season of Europe's elite club competition. The anticipation for the final is heightened by the prospect of witnessing top-tier football talent competing on the ultimate stage. Football enthusiasts and supporters eagerly await the crowning of the 2024 UEFA Champions League winner at this iconic venue.

For more information and updates, please visit UEFA's official website and stay tuned for the latest developments leading up to the 2024 Champions League final.
