July 2024 · 4 minute read

What qualifications do you need to be an Orangetheory coach?

Fitness Coach / Personal Trainer

Must be able to coach a minimum of 10 classes per week. Must hold and maintain a current fitness certification from an OTF approved education company.

How much do OTF coaches make per class?

How much does Orangetheory Fitness in the United States pay? Average Orangetheory Fitness hourly pay ranges from approximately $10.50 per hour for Sales and Service Associate to $40.37 per hour for Coach.

How much does a head trainer at Orangetheory make?

The typical Orangetheory Fitness Head Trainer salary is $44,788. Head Trainer salaries at Orangetheory Fitness can range from $32,727 – $139,640.

What does an orange theory coach do?

Coach. Orangetheory Fitness Coaches are the center of what makes Orangetheory Fitness the best workout in the world. You will be responsible for delivering our workouts to an outstanding standard and navigating the entire class experience from start to finish to suit all fitness levels.

How long until you see results from Orangetheory?

OTF absolutely helps with calorie deficit. Give it a couple weeks (3x per week with good diet) to see a significant a trend on the scale. Do Orangetheory coaches get benefits?

No vacation or benefits. If you don’t conform to the cult-like environment, you won’t advance here. You also won’t be given the best hours and best classes; Head coaches get that. You’re not guaranteed your set schedule every week.

Is it OK to do Orangetheory everyday?

Having said that, doing two Orangetheory workouts in a single day (or doubling up multiple days a week) is strongly not advised! Doing too much, too frequently, without enough rest and recovery however, can have negative physiological impacts and be detrimental to achieving your fitness goals.

Can I use my OTF heart rate monitor outside of class?

Is Orangetheory twice a week enough?

So, yes! Two workouts a week is perfect in the beginning, but I would encourage members to consider adding one day per week for every month you’re a member (if it’s your second month, bump it to three workouts a week, and then in your third month, bump it up to four workouts a week).

Is Orange Theory for Beginners?

If you’re like me and have been terrified to step into a gym, but want to workout, I highly recommend Orange Theory. They offer a free class so you can try it before paying for any classes. If you’re really terrified, try going at a weird time when it’s less busy, or go with a friend.
