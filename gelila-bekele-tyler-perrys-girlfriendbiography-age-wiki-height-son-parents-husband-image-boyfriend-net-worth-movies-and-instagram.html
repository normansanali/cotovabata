Gelila Bekele Career – Wikipedia And Movies

Bekele is a model, social activist, and documentary filmmaker. Bekele spends most of her life shuttling between Europe and the United States. Her modeling career began in 2006 working with Ford Models New York and Ford Models Paris.

Bekele is a devoted advocate for young girls’ right to equal opportunities in her homeland of Ethiopia, as well as globally.
