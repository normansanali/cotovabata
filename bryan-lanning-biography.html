Bryan Lanning Bio (Wiki)

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Who is Bryan Lanning?

Bryan Lanning is an American YouTuber and Country Singer. Lanning is renowned for his YouTube Vlogs on his YouTube Channel.

In 2015, his first song This Is Home was streamed over 9 million times. Currently, he is working on his new music.

He also owns Cali Co. Music.

What are the birth facts of Bryan Lanning?

Bryan Lanning was born on the 20th of January 1990 in California. He is of British ancestry.

He has an elder sister, Bev Lanning.

Early Life

While growing up, he never had a lot and faced poverty. He was born from his mother’s affair which never turned into marriage. Later, he was raised by his grandmother along with her sister in San Diego.

As a child, he was always interested in singing. At the age of 5, he had his songs recorded by his mother. His mother married a man. At the time, he was only 6 years. With his family, he moved away to the mountains where he experienced the country’s life.

When he was in 3rd grade, the choir teacher caught him singing who enrolled in his music classes.

Despite the financial crisis, he attended Boise State University in Boise, ID due to the support from his grandfather. During his University days, he took videography training.

In 2o14, he met his biological father first time due to social media Facebook.

Bryan Lanning- Professional Career

YouTuber

– In 2009, Bryan Lanning moved back to California along with his wife for better job opportunities. In 2012, created a YouTube channel and started vlogging their daily life. His first video was upload on the 27th of January 2013. For the channel, his videography channel came handy.

– In the channel, he shared his life moment’s which are funny, emotional and inspiring the viewers which won the hearts and attention of many people. Due to the channel contents, it turned out a huge success.

Singer

  • On the 20th of January 2017, he released his debut album, Us on his own recording label. The album consists of 14 tracks give a glimpse of his life.
  • Prior to that, in 2016, he released the extended play, Like a Lion with 5 tracks.
  • Next, on the 24th of January 2020, he is releasing his next country album, Illegal under the recording label, Cali Co. Music.
