Meet Savannah Gabriel! Her Bio, Age, Family, Job

August 2024 · 5 minute read

MTV’s newest reality series, Buckhead Shore, focuses on a group of established friends as they spend the summer at a family lake house in Buckhead, Georgia aka the “Beverly Hills of the South.” You can expect some high-drama hookups, wild nights, and fun times from the show. Savannah Gabriel, one of the cast members of the new MTV show, is in a very tricky situation since she is living with her boyfriend and his ex under the same roof.

Does Savannah have what it takes to manage her relationship with her boyfriend while still being around her boyfriend’s ex? Find that out, as well as information about her age, family, and job, in the article below.

Savannah Gabriel On Buckhead Shore

Buckhead Shore premiered on June 23rd, 2022 and you can watch every episode of the show on Thursdays at 9 pm EST on MTV. Unlike shows like Jersey Shore and Floribama shore, wherein a group of strangers is thrown under the same roof, Buckhead Shore’s cast were already friends.

While the cast has a certain level of a bond between them, no one can guarantee that some of the longtime relationships won’t break during their time at the lakehouse.

Besides Savannah Gabriel, all the other cast members of Buckhead Shore are JuJu Barney, Katie Canham, Adamo Giraldo, Parker Lipman, Bethania Locke, Pat Muresan, Chelsea Prescott, and DJ Simmons.

Recently, in an interview with the OK Magazine, the cast revealed that when they all signed up to star on Buckhead Shore they had no idea what they were in for.

“We thought it was going to be a cakewalk, but it was a very cool experience,” Parker Lipman, Savannah’s boyfriend told OK!

Savannah’s bio on the MTV website tells that she has only lived in Buckhead for a few years now. Despite her short time in Buckhead, she has already started “making waves” in her friend group, thanks to her sweet yet spicy personality. Inside the lakehouse, not only does she need to manage her relatively new relationship with her boyfriend Parker but also handle dealing with Parker’s ex-girlfriend, Katie Canham.

After the premiere of the show, Savannah took to Instagram and explained how grateful she feels to have been a part of Buckhead Shore. She wrote, “beyond grateful to be a part of @buckheadshore & to have had the best summer with friends I now consider family,” sharing some of the pictures from the show.

Is Savannah Gabriel Still Dating Parker Lipman?

Savannah Gabriel’s boyfriend, Parker Lipman aka the head buck of the group is a self-proclaimed mama’s boy. His family owns the Zaxby’s chicken fast-food chain and he hosts a summer getaway for his friends every year at his family’s lake house.

Parker dated his now-ex-girlfriend, Katie Canham, for 4 years. Katie, who likes to call herself the “queen bae” of the group, recently lost her mother. She’s looking for a fresh start in the lakehouse. Will it be the case considering she will have her ex-Parker and his new girl Savannah around her 24/7?

Although we did not know if Savannah and Parker were still together after the filming of the show, Savannah did admit she was ‘nervous’ sharing a common roof with her beau Parker’s ex Katie.

Buckhead Shore’s Savannah Admits she was ‘Nervous’ to stay at Lake House with BF Parker’s Ex Katie. “Going into it, I was super nervous and hesitant, and then I think once I got there and saw Parker and saw Katie and there was alcohol involved, I definitely was more on the confrontational side,” she said.

Talking about Savannah’s past relationships, she dated a man named Mike for 7 years. Unfortunately, Mike is not in this world anymore and Savannah made a heartfelt post about his passing on Instagram on January 27, 2018.

Sharing some of their memorable pictures, she wrote, “mike, I can’t believe you are really gone. when I first heard the news I couldn’t even believe it…. you were my youth and my young adulthood… Rest in peace sweet angel”

Savannah Gabriel Family

Coming to Savannah Gabriel’s family, her parents are Kristy Gabriel (mother) and Dean S Gabriel (father).

In 2016, Savannah’s mom, Kristy, passed out and was completely unconscious. In the hospital, she was diagnosed with a ruptured brain aneurysm and doctors suggested getting brain surgery asap.

Kristy did make great progress with the help of surgery. However, she did not have any health insurances to cover the medical bills. Savannah organized a fundraiser through GoFundMe and raised $9,120 out of $10,000 (goal).

Buckhead Shore also has a sister named Katie Gabriel in her family.

Savannah Gabriel Height
