Coco Austin on Breastfeeding: My Nipples Feel Like They Are Gonna Fall Off

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Nicole “Coco” Austin is already celebrating the joys — and difficulties — of motherhood. The new mom, who gave birth to daughter Chanel Nicole on Saturday, Nov. 28, dished on the soreness from breastfeeding in a new post on Facebook.

“My nipples feel like they are gonna fall off,” Austin, 36, wrote on Sunday, Nov. 29. “They’re so raw from breastfeeding #NewMommyproblems.”

Luckily, this is the only disadvantage that Coco has had to endure so far. She assured her fans that the birth of her new baby went perfectly according to plan.

“Sorry for the silence but still in hospital after giving birth yesterday,” the Ice Loves Coco star wrote in the post. “I’ve had my hands full focusing on being the best mother I can be! I’m so freaking in love. I’ve had an angel watch over me during my whole pregnancy and labor cuz it went beautiful every step of the way.”

Austin and her husband, Law & Order: Special Victims Unit star Ice T, announced Chanel’s arrival over the weekend with an adorable photo of their new bundle of joy.

“Surprise!!! Look who came early!” she wrote. “Welcome the new arrival of Chanel Nicole… A beautiful, healthy 5.7 pound baby girl.”
